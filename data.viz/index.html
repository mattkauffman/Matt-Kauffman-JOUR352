Analysis of MLB Payroll Effect on Winning

By Matt Kauffman

The first of the following graphs displays the correlation between total payroll (salary of active/inactive players) and team wins for the 2024 season. The Mets topped the salary charts spending $317 million, with the Athletics in last at $62 million.
The second graph displays the active payroll of the last 10 World Series champions. These analytics provide some insight into the correlation between spending and winning across the MLB, a league without a salary cap. Generally, higher spending teams are more successful, but only to a certain extent. While nearly all of the last 10 WS winners were in the top half of the league payroll, only half were in the top 5.
